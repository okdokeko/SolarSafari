Getting Started

Many people have wondered what this rocky planet will have in store for them. Well you're in luck! The Solar Safari cruise ship will take us sailing right on by for a once in a lifetime view! Here, you will get to experience the wonders of of a beutiful deep gray formed from the oceans of lava that spent millions of years cooling! Between that and the intricate white streaks formed from craters it is an awe inspiring sight! You will aslo get the chance to look through the lens of old Earth astronomers using a special chemical map to portray chemical concententrations on the surface!
